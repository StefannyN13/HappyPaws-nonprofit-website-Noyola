How you can help

Local animal shelters work hard every day to care for animals in need. This donation drive helps provide essential supplies that shelters use daily. Your contributions help ensure that animals have food, comfort, and a safe place to stay while they wait for their forever homes.

There are several ways you can help. The most impactful way is by donating supplies that shelters use regularly.

Most Needed Items

Animal shelters rely on donations to keep their animals healthy and comfortable. The following items are especially helpful:

  • Blankets and towels
  • Dog and cat food
  • Food and water bowls
  • Pet beds
  • Pet toys
  • Small rugs or mats

Dog Supplies

Items for dogs are always needed. You can help by donating:

  • Dry dog food (sealed bags only)
  • Canned dog food
  • Dog treats of any type
  • Dog toys
  • Leashes and collars
  • Dog beds
  • Food and water bowls
  • No-pull harnesses
  • Collars
  • Biodegradable poop bags

Cat Supplies

Cats make up a large portion of animals in shelters. The following items are very helpful:

  • Dry cat food (sealed bags only)
  • Canned cat food
  • Cat treats of any type
  • Cat toys
  • Scratching posts or pads
  • Cat carriers
  • Food and water bowls
  • Cat litter

Items We cannot Accept

Some items cannot be used by shelters. Please avoid donating:

  • Expired pet food
  • Opened bags of pet food
  • Broken toys
  • Damaged pet carriers
