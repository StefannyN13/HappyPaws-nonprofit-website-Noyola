# HappyPaws-nonprofit-website-Noyola

## Mission Statement
Happy Paws is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting local animal shelters in the Will County area through donation drives. These drives collect essential items such as pet food, toys, blankets, towels, and accessories.

## Website Purpose
The purpose of this website is to provide clear information about the donation drive, including how and what to donate, drop-off locations, and volunteer opportunities.

## Target Audience
- Will County community members
- Pet owners
- Animal lovers
- Potential donors and sponsors
- Individuals interested in volunteering 

## Planned Features

- Home page with a mission overview and a clear call to action, “Donate Supplies,” which redirects users to the “What to Donate” section
- About the Drive section explaining how the drive started and how donations support local shelters
- What to Donate section listing accepted and non-accepted items
- Drop-Off Locations section displaying donation dates and locations
- Get Involved section outlining available volunteer opportunities and including a contact form
- Donate section allowing users to contribute through online payments.
- Contact Us section with a basic contact form, email address, phone number, and social media information
- Mobile-friendly responsive design
